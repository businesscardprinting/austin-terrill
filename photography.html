East Germany
2017

Set in the Dübener Heide of Saxony-Anhalt, this photo series captures the stark beauty and quiet desolation of an area steeped in over 1,000 years of history. Once a thriving medieval forest region, it has seen the rise and fall of empires. 



Over the past century, it bore the scars of both world wars and the East German era under Soviet rule. After reunification, much of East Germany, including Dübener Heide, faced economic decline and depopulation, leaving behind an eerie stillness—a landscape suspended between past grandeur and present abandonment.

Völkerschlachtdenkmal
2022

The Völkerschlachtdenkmal, standing in solemn grandeur, commemorates the 1813 Battle of Leipzig, a turning point in the Napoleonic Wars. Its imposing figures evoke sacrifice, strength, and the complex history of German nationalism. 

Once embraced by militaristic ideologies, today it reminds us of the cost of war and the fragility of peace. My sepia-toned photos capture the monument’s timeless, haunting power, reflecting both the weight of history and the quiet resilience of remembrance.

Çıralı
2022

"Çıralı" showcases the ancient ruins of Olympus, nestled along Turkey's Mediterranean coast. Once a vibrant Lycian city over 2,000 years ago, Olympus flourished as a center of trade and culture before falling into decline under Roman and Byzantine rule. 





Over centuries, pirates, earthquakes, and invasions left it abandoned. Today, its serene, crumbling beauty is a testament to the ebb and flow of civilizations, with ongoing debates about preservation and modern tourism’s impact. 

Forbidden Love
2023

This photo series captures the raw, intimate story of a Muslim woman and a Christian man, navigating their love in the vibrant, open landscape of modern Berlin. Having left behind their conservative roots, they explore a new paradigm of connection

—one that defies the boundaries of tradition and religion.
Through their vulnerability, this series reflects the complexities of forbidden love, freedom, and the deep emotional exploration of identity in a changing world. 

Hotel
2024

"Hotel" captures the passionate, secret rendezvous of two lovers who meet in the secluded countryside of East Germany. Set against the backdrop of a 4-day hedonistic music festival, their affair contrasts sharply with the serenity of a health spa hotel. 

This series explores the tension between indulgence and tranquility, as their forbidden romance unfolds in a space where desire and escape from reality collide amidst the quiet stillness of rural East Germany.
